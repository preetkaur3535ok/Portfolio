Efficiently Allocating Huge Pages Based on Data Temperature in Virtualized Clouds (HOTTLB)
Zhaoxi Shi · Jiyuan Zhang · Jianchen Shan (Hofstra University) · Weiwei Jia · Preet Kaur

HOTTLB proposes an effective solution that allocates huge pages based on the data temperature of applications in virtualized systems. Addressing memory fragmentation and TLB miss overhead in cloud environments through intelligent hot-data prioritization. Research conducted on EPT/TLB behavior in x86 hypervisors, with direct implications for secure cloud infrastructure design and multi-tenant virtualization security.
